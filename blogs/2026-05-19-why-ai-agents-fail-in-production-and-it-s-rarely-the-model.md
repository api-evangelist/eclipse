Why AI agents fail in production (and it’s rarely the model) AI agents rarely fail because their models are not “smart enough”. In open source AI systems, failures typically occur later, when demos are deployed to production. In early demos, a single large language model (LLM), a clean prompt loop, and a cloud endpoint are often enough.
