Open Community for AI at OCX26 This recap covers the Open Community for AI track at Open Community Experience 2026 (OCX26), with sessions focused on AI agents, edge AI, domain-specific systems, security, and the role of open source in building production-ready AI systems. Across three days at OCX26, the Open Community for AI stayed grounded in one question: what actually holds up when AI moves from demo to production? The focus of these sessions was on constraints, trade-offs, and system design.
