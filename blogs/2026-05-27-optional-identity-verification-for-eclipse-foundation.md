Optional Identity Verification for Eclipse Foundation Committers Launches June 2 Trust is a core part of open source collaboration. At the Eclipse Foundation, we have always required committers to provide their real first and last name when signing their committer agreements, and we have long stated that certain committer information, including name and email address, is publicly displayed in connection with contributions. On June 2 , the Eclipse Foundation will make optional identity verification generally available for Eclipse Foundation committers.
