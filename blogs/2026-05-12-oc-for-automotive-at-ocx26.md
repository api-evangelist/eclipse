OC for Automotive at OCX26 The Open Community for Automotive at OCX26 focused on how open source collaboration is transforming the software-defined vehicle ecosystem and enabling a full middleware platform for series vehicle production. Across three days, the track moved from orchestration and operating system design to safety, cybersecurity, and real-world deployment. The discussions stayed grounded in implementation, following the mantra of “code-first”.
