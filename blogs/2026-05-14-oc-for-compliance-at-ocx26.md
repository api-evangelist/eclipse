OC for Compliance at OCX26 This recap covers the Open Community for Compliance track at Open Community Experience 2026 (OCX26), with sessions focused on the Cyber Resilience Act (CRA), SBOMs, open source governance, and what compliance actually requires from engineering teams. Across three days at OCX26, the Open Community for Compliance track stayed grounded in one reality: compliance is no longer a legal discussion happening somewhere else. It is now shaping how software is designed, built, and maintained.
